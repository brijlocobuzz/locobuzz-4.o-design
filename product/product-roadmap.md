# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Home
Composable role-based dashboard with pinnable blocks. Role-specific defaults for Agent, Marketer, Analyst, and Admin personas.

### 2. Mentions
Raw mention stream showing all social and web conversations about the brand with AI-powered enrichment (sentiment, intent, classifications).

### 3. Inbox
Ticket management workspace with secondary sidebar for filtered views (New, Open, Recently Closed), assignment filters (Assigned to Me, My Team, Unassigned), and custom queues.

### 4. Brand Posts
Published content management with secondary sidebar for channel filters (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google Business).

### 5. Alerts
Real-time alert feed for spikes, anomalies, SLA breaches, and important brand events requiring attention.

### 6. Workflows
No-code workflow builder for automation rules, AI agent configuration, and work handoff integrations with external systems.

### 7. Insights
Qualitative insights including trending topics, emerging themes, audience questions, and AI-generated summaries of brand conversations.

### 8. Dashboards
Composable analytics dashboards for sentiment trends, competitive benchmarking, performance metrics, and custom reports.

### 9. Chat with Data
AI-powered conversational interface for exploring data, asking questions, and generating insights without building reports.

### 10. Settings
Brand setup, social channel connections, AI enrichment rules, custom classifications, user/team management, and workspace preferences.
